WHO ARE WE ?

Zimelo Pty(Ltd) is a professional and innovative construction company. The company provides multi-disciplinary services in the construction field. Zimelo Pty(Ltd) was established in 2016 with the three variables of project management (cost, time and quantity).

"Constructing the Future, Today."

Highly Experienced and Qualified

Our experience and professional approach in both the public and private sector has enabled us to make our mark in the industry.

We pride ourselves in the experience of our key human resources who contribute in providing integrated solutions that are responsive to a demanding construction environment.
